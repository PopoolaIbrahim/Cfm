Instant Gear Swap!

Use Command:/replaceitem entity @p slot.hotbar.0 minecraft:diamond_sword

Gear up for adventure with /replaceitem! This command lets you instantly replace an item in your hotbar with a shiny new diamond sword. No need to fumble through your inventory – just execute this command, and you're ready to face any challenges that come your way. Whether you're battling mobs or other players, make sure your inventory is always equipped with the best tools for the job! ⚔️
